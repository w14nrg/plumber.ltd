What Is an SLA?

A Service Level Agreement (SLA) is a contract between Chelsea Plumbers and your retail business. It guarantees regular maintenance, fast emergency responses, and predictable costs to keep your shop running smoothly. Clients must provide timely access to premises for inspections and repairs to ensure SLA compliance. Benefits include:

  • Prevent costly breakdowns with regular inspections
  • Priority service for emergencies (e.g., burst pipes, minor blockages)
  • Save money compared to one-off repair costs
  • Ensure compliance with regulations (e.g., gas safety checks)

We commit to agreed response times and services; you pay a fixed monthly or annual fee. Contact us for a sample contract.

All SLA Plans Include:

  • Annual gas safety checks (if applicable)
  • Discounted repairs (10-20% off £90-£120/hour rates for non-covered work)
  • Free initial site check for custom quote
  • Rolling monthly or annual contract (5% discount for annual upfront payment)
  • 24/7 emergency line

Response Times & Emergency Fees:

  • Emergencies (e.g., bursts, minor blockages): Vary by plan—faster on higher tiers.
  • If faster response needed than plan allows, add £150 emergency upgrade fee (waived on higher plans).
  • Routine issues: Next business day scheduling on all plans.

Terms of Agreement

  • Coverage: SLA plans cover normal wear and tear (e.g., tap fixes, minor blockages like sink or toilet clogs cleared by plunger/rod, toilet cistern repairs). Excludes pre-existing issues, client negligence (e.g., flushing non-flushable items), major installations (e.g., full pipe systems, new boilers), or complex repairs requiring specialized equipment (e.g., jetting, excavation, or non-standard parts). Membership plans provide discounts and priority service; all work charged at fixed rates (£108/£102/£90/hour for Basic/Standard/Premium) plus parts at cost.
  • Caps: Silver SLA covers parts up to £500/year for minor repairs; Gold SLA covers parts and labor up to £2,000/year for repairs (not installations or complex repairs). Membership plans have no repair caps.
  • Fair Use: Silver’s 4 and Gold’s 6 minor callouts/year (1 hour free labor each) are subject to fair use; excessive use may trigger review.
  • Inspections: SLA inspections limited to 1 hour each; additional time charged at £90-£120/hour. Monthly SLA contracts include prorated inspections (none for Bronze, 1 every 3 months for Silver, 1 every 6 weeks for Gold). Premium membership includes one free 1-hour inspection per year; additional inspections charged at £90-£120/hour.
  • Response Times: Guaranteed as listed (24/8/4 hours for SLA plans, 48/12/4 hours for membership plans). £150 fee for faster response than plan allows (waived on higher plans). Missed SLAs due to unforeseen circumstances (e.g., extreme weather, staff unavailability) may result in a £50 service credit, at our discretion.
  • Overages: SLA labor beyond 1 hour/callout or inspection, or repairs beyond caps, charged at £90-£120/hour (10-20% off for non-covered work, varies by job complexity or timing) plus parts at cost. Membership work charged at fixed rates (£108/£102/£90/hour for Basic/Standard/Premium) plus parts at cost. See our FAQ for rate details.
  • Client Responsibility: Clients must maintain systems (e.g., avoid misuse) and provide timely access to ensure plan compliance. Unauthorized system modifications void coverage unless approved by Chelsea Plumbers.
  • Payment Terms: Rolling monthly plans (SLA or membership) require first month’s payment upfront and 30-day cancellation notice. Annual SLA contracts require upfront payment with 5% discount. Late payments incur a £25 fee or 5% of the outstanding balance, whichever is higher.
  • Reviews & Cancellation: Annual SLA contracts have a minimum 12-month term, reviewed every 6 months; 30 days’ notice required for cancellation. Rolling monthly plans (SLA or membership) cancellable with 30 days’ notice.
  • Pricing: Prices are for standard shops. Chelsea Plumbers may adjust pricing post-inspection.
  • Full Terms: Detailed contract available upon request—email us.
